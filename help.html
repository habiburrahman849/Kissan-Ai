Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a crop disease?
Go to the Chat page and tap the attachment (+) button, then send a clear photo of the affected leaf/stem. Describe symptoms in text as well. Kissan AI will use your farm memory and local guidance to suggest possible next steps. For serious outbreaks, always confirm with a local extension officer — photo diagnosis is advisory, not a lab result.
Can I use voice commands in regional languages?
Yes! Kissan AI supports voice input in Urdu, Hinglish / Roman Urdu, and English. Go to Settings > Language to set your preferred voice command language. Tap the microphone button and speak your question.
How accurate are the weather forecasts?
Our weather data comes from Pakistan Meteorological Department (PMD) and additional satellite sources, with 95%+ accuracy for 3-day forecasts. Advisories are tailored to your specific crops and location for maximum relevance.
How do I update my farm details?
Go to Profile page and click the "Edit" button on the Farm Details card. You can update land size, crops, soil type, irrigation method, and farming type. Changes are saved instantly.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. All your data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL. We never share your farm data with third parties. You can control visibility settings in your profile. Your conversations are stored securely.
